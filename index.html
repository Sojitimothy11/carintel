ABOUT THE AUTHOR

My name is Lasisi Oluwadunsin David, and I am fundraising for my authored book, "Career Intelligence: Guide to Building Career Intelligence and Making Progress." As an experienced IT professional with a strong foundation in Information Technologies and a diverse background in areas spanning project management, product management, and administration, I have had the privilege of witnessing firsthand the significance of career intelligence. Eager to share my knowledge and experience, I have decided to share some deep insights through this book, which I believe will be a valuable resource for individuals seeking to make informed career decisions and achieve progress in their professional journeys.

ABOUT THE BOOK

I have observed talented individuals struggling to find their true calling or make progress in their chosen paths due to a lack of guidance and understanding about their own capabilities. This book is capable of empowering countless individuals on their journey toward personal and professional fulfillment.
